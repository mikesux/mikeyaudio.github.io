About

Mike's roots are planted in Cape Breton Island, a region with a long history of talent and culture. He grew up in a tech savvy & musical family, where he was encouraged to experiment and find his own voice. As a result, he became a multi-instrumentalist, & tech-tinkerer in his early teens, which led him on a self-directed learning path to achieve many accomplishments. Mike is currently working on project & curriculum development for UIT Startup Immersion.

Mike has significant experience across graphic design, animation, programming, computer hardware and sound design. In the past Mike has worked at Wingit Studios as a Sound Engineer, Collideascope Digital as an Animator and Graphic Designer (where his work was broadcasted nationally),and as a globally touring professional musician. Mike is the founder of musicAbility, a music therapy program that helps at-risk youth.
